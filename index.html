About Byron

Who Am I?

You have a question about who is Byron? you are in the right place.Well,Born on 20 May 1997, Byron is a Zimbabwean Techno-preneur who depicted that it was his responsibility to raise the standard of technology and emancipation in the eyes of the nation and he was nicknamed THE EMANCIPATOR later on.At the age of 20 Byron cofounded an ICT Based firm called THE BAYZ and rebranded it to New Wave Zim in 2019(The year he set the idea into action).Late 2019 Byron introduced a subsidiary of New Wave Zim called My Kingdom.

Apart from being a Techno-prenuer, Byron is an acclaimed web blogger and presenter at Ny Television, Brand ambassador at Oryx Watch, web developer,mobile developer,creative content designer and Artificial intelligence fanatic. In short Byron is an Information and Technology guru having all the experience in Software engineering, Computer hardware engineering and Networking engineering. In education, Byron is a holder of Information technology and python programming credentials having enrolled with Kwekwe Institute of engineering,science and technology and internationally enrolled with Udemy. Socially, Byron is a social influencer,having participated in different social discussions for example he once participated in the United Nations Development Program for digital transformation. Politically, Byron is not publicly active in any political affairs and in religion Byron Mukushwa is a Christian by birth.
